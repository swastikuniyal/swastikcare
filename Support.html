Common Questions

How to book a follow-up?
Go to the 'Appointment' tab on the home screen, select your doctor, and choose an available slot.
Where can I find my reports?
All your reports are securely stored in the 'Health Records' section on the main dashboard.
Is video consultation available?
Yes, you can use the 'Talk to Doctor' feature to start a chat or request a video call.
