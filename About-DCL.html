Project Design Process

Course Code: CSCR1504 (DCL)

Problem Statement

The Gap

Post-surgery recovery is often neglected due to poor communication between patients and doctors. Many patients lack a clear roadmap for their recovery journey.

Proposed Solution

Swastik Care

A digital ecosystem that provides a 'Move On Stronger' roadmap. It integrates vitals tracking, instant doctor consultations, and recovery milestones in one mobile portal.

Design Journey

Creativity Lab

Designed at Pandey Traders Lab, focusing on accessibility for rural and urban patients. The UI is built using a mobile-first approach for ease of use by all age groups.
